Print Preview Instructions

To test the hierarchical layout in print preview:

  1. Press Ctrl+P (or Cmd+P on Mac) to open print preview
  2. Verify that typography hierarchy is clear and readable
  3. Check that strategic whitespace creates proper visual separation
  4. Ensure multi-column layouts maintain alignment
  5. Confirm that attention guides and visual flow elements are visible
  6. Validate that content groups have proper spacing and organization
